# Problem-Solving Approach

This project demonstrates an end-to-end analytics workflow focused on business decision-making.

---

## Step 1: Business Understanding
Defined clear business questions related to:
- Revenue growth
- Funnel efficiency
- Marketing performance
- Experiment impact
- Customer and product value

---

## Step 2: Data Preparation
- Designed normalized tables for customers, products, campaigns, events, and transactions.
- Converted timestamps into DATETIME format.
- Cleaned invalid values and standardized numeric fields.
- Created analytics-ready SQL views for reporting.

---

## Step 3: Data Validation
- Checked for null values, duplicates, and orphan records.
- Verified date ranges for events and transactions.
- Ensured joins between behavioral and transactional data were reliable.

---

## Step 4: Analysis & Modeling
- Built funnel logic using event-level data.
- Linked marketing and experiment exposure to revenue outcomes.
- Calculated conversion rates, AOV, revenue per user, and growth trends.

---

## Step 5: Visualization & Storytelling
- Designed Power BI dashboards aligned with business questions.
- Structured dashboard flow from executive overview to deep insights.
- Focused on clarity, relevance, and decision-oriented metrics.

---

## Outcome
This project shows the ability to:
- Translate raw data into business insights
- Apply structured analytical thinking
- Communicate findings clearly to stakeholders
